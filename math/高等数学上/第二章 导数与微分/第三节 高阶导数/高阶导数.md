__莱布尼兹公式__ 
$$ (u + v)^n = \sum_{k=0}^nC_n^ku^{n-k}v^k $$

#### 常用高阶导数
$ (e^x)^{(n)} = e^x $
$ (\sin{x})^{(n)} = \sin(x+\frac{n\pi}{2}) $
$ (\cos{x})^{(n)} = \cos(x+\frac{n\pi}{2}) $
$ (\ln(1+x))^{(n)} = (-1)^{n-1}\frac{(n-1)!}{(1+x)^n} $
$ (x^u)^{(n)} = \left\{ 
    \begin{aligned} 
    C_n^kx^{(u-k)} , (u \ge k), \\ 
    0, (u < k),
    \end{aligned} 
    \right. $
